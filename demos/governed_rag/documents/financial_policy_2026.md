# Financial Advisory Policy 2026

## Suitability Framework

All investment recommendations must be evaluated against the client's risk
profile, investment objectives, time horizon, and liquidity needs before
delivery.

### Conservative Client Profiles

Conservative clients (risk score 1-3) are suitable for:
- Investment-grade fixed income (BBB or above)
- Municipal bonds (general obligation and revenue)
- Diversified equity index funds with low turnover
- Money market instruments and Treasury securities

A balanced 60/40 portfolio (60% fixed income, 40% equity) is the standard
allocation for conservative profiles with a 5+ year horizon.

### Moderate Client Profiles

Moderate clients (risk score 4-6) may additionally hold:
- High-yield corporate bonds (up to 15% of fixed income allocation)
- International developed market equity (up to 20% of equity allocation)
- Real estate investment trusts (REITs, up to 10% of portfolio)

### Aggressive Client Profiles

Aggressive clients (risk score 7-10) have broader latitude including:
- Emerging market equity and debt
- Alternative investments (hedge funds, private equity) with appropriate
  accreditation and documentation
- Sector-concentrated positions (with documented rationale)

## Documentation Requirements

Every recommendation must include:
1. Client risk profile assessment date and score
2. Suitability rationale linked to specific policy sections
3. Fee disclosure and impact analysis
4. Conflict of interest statement
