# Regulatory Compliance Manual

## Regulation Best Interest (Reg BI)

Under SEC Regulation Best Interest, all recommendations must satisfy:

1. **Disclosure Obligation**: Provide Form CRS and fee schedule before or
   at the time of recommendation.
2. **Care Obligation**: Exercise reasonable diligence, care, and skill.
   Consider the investment's risks, rewards, and costs. Consider
   reasonably available alternatives.
3. **Conflict of Interest Obligation**: Establish, maintain, and enforce
   written policies to identify and disclose or eliminate all conflicts.
4. **Compliance Obligation**: Establish and maintain policies reasonably
   designed to achieve compliance with Reg BI.

## FINRA Rule 2111 — Suitability

Three components:
- **Reasonable-basis suitability**: adequate understanding of the product
- **Customer-specific suitability**: suitable for the particular customer
- **Quantitative suitability**: not excessive given customer profile

## FINRA Rule 3110 — Supervision

All investment recommendations must be reviewed by a supervisor within
the prescribed timeframe:
- Standard recommendations: 5 business days
- Complex products: 2 business days
- High-risk or concentrated positions: same business day

## Record Retention

All recommendation records, suitability analyses, and client communications
must be retained for a minimum of 6 years per SEC Rule 17a-4.

## Escalation Procedures

Escalate to compliance immediately when:
- A recommendation involves a restricted instrument
- Client profile data is incomplete or outdated (> 12 months)
- A potential conflict of interest is identified
- System integrity or data quality concerns arise
