# Restricted and Prohibited Instruments

## Prohibited Instruments (Hard Stop)

The following instruments are PROHIBITED for all client profiles and must
never appear in any recommendation:

1. **Leveraged ETFs** (2x, 3x, inverse) — unsuitable for buy-and-hold;
   daily reset creates tracking error that compounds losses.
2. **Binary options** — speculative instruments with total loss risk.
3. **Unregistered securities** — cannot be recommended without SEC
   registration or valid exemption.
4. **Cryptocurrency spot positions** — not approved for advisory accounts
   pending regulatory clarity.

## Restricted Instruments (Compliance Review Required)

The following require compliance pre-approval:
1. **Options strategies** — covered calls permitted for aggressive profiles
   only with documented experience assessment.
2. **Structured products** — require suitability review and written
   client acknowledgment of complexity and liquidity risk.
3. **Private placements** — accredited investor verification required.
4. **Margin accounts** — must document client understanding of margin
   risk and maintenance requirements.

## Instrument-Specific Suitability Rules

- Municipal bonds: appropriate for taxable accounts; verify state-specific
  tax status.
- High-yield bonds: maximum 15% of fixed income for moderate profiles;
  prohibited for conservative profiles.
- International investments: currency risk disclosure required for all
  non-USD denominated positions.

## Compliance Override Process

Any recommendation involving restricted instruments must include:
1. Compliance officer pre-approval (written)
2. Client risk acknowledgment (signed)
3. Suitability override documentation with rationale
