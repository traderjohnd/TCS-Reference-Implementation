# Investment Guidelines

## Asset Allocation Standards

### Target Allocations by Risk Profile

| Profile       | Equity | Fixed Income | Alternatives | Cash |
|---------------|--------|-------------|--------------|------|
| Conservative  | 30-40% | 50-60%      | 0-5%         | 5-10% |
| Moderate      | 50-60% | 30-40%      | 5-10%        | 5%   |
| Aggressive    | 70-80% | 10-20%      | 10-15%       | 5%   |

### Rebalancing Policy

Portfolios should be rebalanced when any asset class deviates more than
5 percentage points from its target allocation. Rebalancing reviews occur
quarterly at minimum.

### Concentration Limits

No single equity position may exceed 5% of total portfolio value for
conservative and moderate profiles. Aggressive profiles may hold up to
10% in a single position with documented rationale.

### Duration Management

Fixed income duration should be matched to the client's time horizon:
- Short-term (< 3 years): duration 1-3 years
- Intermediate (3-7 years): duration 3-7 years
- Long-term (> 7 years): duration 7-12 years

## Liquidity Requirements

Maintain minimum 5% cash or cash equivalents for all profiles.
Conservative profiles should maintain 10% for near-term needs.

## Tax Efficiency

Tax-loss harvesting should be evaluated quarterly. Municipal bonds
should be prioritized for taxable accounts in high tax brackets.
